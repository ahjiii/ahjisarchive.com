In San Francisco

At Garden Alchemy, we know that chateau is just a fancy word for house. That's why we're delighted to extend a warm invitation to our haven nestled in the heart of San Francisco. While our wines have always been a product of this vibrant city, we've embarked on a new chapter, establishing deeper roots in our urban landscape. Our tasting room, unveiled in 2018 and meticulously crafted by C. Walters Design, radiates modernity, warmth, and an ambiance dedicated to cherishing life's simple pleasures alongside our intricate wines. And here's an added incentive to join us: our exclusive Californian wines, available only through our Tasting Room, Wine Club, and Online Shop, await your discovery.
